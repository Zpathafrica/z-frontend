— Why this exists

Your money has no privacy.

Zcash changes that. Shielded transactions are mathematically private — verified on-chain, invisible to everyone except you. ZPath is how you learn to use them.

I

Mobile money sees everything.

Every transaction is recorded. Who you paid, how much, when. Accessible to operators, authorities, and partners.

II

Data leaks happen.

Financial records are regularly exposed in breaches across the region. Once leaked, your history is permanent.

III

Crypto is not private.

Bitcoin and most digital money leave a public ledger anyone can read. Privacy is the exception, not the default.
